Installation
-------------------

1. Extract and copy the contents of the wso2silfi package to the Drupal
   "modules/custom" directory.

2. Go to the Modules administration page and enable the WSO2Silfi module.

3. Go to "Configuration > People > WSO2 Auth Settings" and enable the
   checkbox "Enable WSO2" and compiled the necessary fields.

4. Go to "Structure > Block layout" and add the login blocks.
